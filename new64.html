Claimed External Account

Note: A claimed external account is a profile or service that has been verified and linked to your identity by our system.

Why Claim Your External Accounts?

Claiming your external accounts ensures:

  • Better security through verified connections.
  • Protection from impersonation or unauthorized use.
  • Improved integration with third-party services.
  • Access to exclusive account-related features.

How We Verify Your External Account

We follow a strict verification process:

  • Account ownership confirmation via email or secure login.
  • Data validation with the connected platform.
  • Activity checks to ensure authenticity.

Security Measures

Your claimed account is protected with:

  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) support.
  • Encrypted data transfers.
  • Regular security audits.

Managing Claimed Accounts

You can manage, unlink, or delete claimed accounts anytime through your Account Settings page.
