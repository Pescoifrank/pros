Privacy Rights

Protecting your personal data in the digital world

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Rights page explains how we protect, use, and manage your personal data across our technology areas: Cyber Security, Programming, Information Science, Software Development, and Computer Networks & Communication.

1. Cyber Security

We use advanced encryption, secure authentication, and network monitoring tools to ensure your data is safe from unauthorized access. You have the right to know how your information is secured and to request additional details about our protection measures.

2. Programming

All code we develop adheres to privacy-by-design principles. This means your personal data is integrated with security in mind from the start, and you have the right to opt out of unnecessary data collection.

3. Information Science

We ensure that data collection and organization follow ethical and legal guidelines. You have the right to request corrections or deletion of inaccurate or outdated data stored in our systems.

4. Software Development

Our applications are built with secure coding practices to safeguard personal data. You have the right to transparency on how the software processes and stores your information.

5. Computer Network & Communication

We maintain secure communication channels to protect your interactions online. You have the right to request encryption for sensitive communications and to be informed about how your messages are handled.

Your Privacy Rights

  • Right to access and review your personal data.
  • Right to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to delete your personal data where applicable.
  • Right to restrict or object to data processing.
  • Right to be informed of data breaches promptly.
